What is Geek on a bike?

Geek on a bike is a local tech support business located in Eugene, Oregon. It was founded to help small business owners deal with all the technical difficulties that occur in this modern age. What sets us apart from other technical support companies is our focus on honesty, courtesy, and convenience for our customers. We are the only tech support business in Eugene that specializes in face to face tech support. We understand the value of doing business the old fashioned way in order to keep things simple as possible in an increasingly complex world. For that reason the services we offer are all based on getting a geek out to your business so they can physically help you. Same day appointments are our bread and butter. Got a problem? Don't worry, a geek will be right there!
